Go-to-Market Strategy: A Phased & Focused Approach

Our launch strategy is designed to build momentum, capture key markets, and scale efficiently.

01

Phase 1 (Year 1): Metro Launch & Brand Build

Launch in the top 10 metro cities. Focus on creating a strong brand narrative through a digital-first campaign. Establish flagship experience centers and leverage online pre-bookings to create hype and demand.
